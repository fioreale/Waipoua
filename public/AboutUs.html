Waipoua Association

What we do

Waipoua is a voluntary association that wants to build a world where humanity can live in harmony with nature. We are sustained by citizens and organization donations and we contribute to the preservation of the natural ecosystems in the world.
Our work is founded on the strong belief that we cannot survive without our planet, our mother earth. We have developed several projects aimed at saving animal species, protecting the forests and fight environmental pollution.
