Why should I donate

The amazing donors who give directly to Waipuoa are what we call builders extraordinaire. They invest in building a larger ecosystem of activism and support that engages more people in our shared mission. Their support helps us to continue building a global network that is not only increasing environmental giving, but also educating the network about becoming more strategic and effective with their giving. We’re a small but scrappy team fueling a large global network of businesses, individuals and nonprofits working for a healthy planet.
Help us keep the momentum going so we can grow our operations, our brand awareness, our movement and our impact. We consider our donors a critical part of our movement, and we’re thankful for your support.
